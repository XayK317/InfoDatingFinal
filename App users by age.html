Dating App Age Demographics (2018–2024)

Tinder

Tinder has consistently attracted a younger audience, particularly users between 18 and 34. Over the years, however, its user base has gradually aged, with noticeable growth in the 35–44 and 55+ age groups.

Source: DataGlobeHub, Business of Apps

Bumble

Bumble's user demographics have diversified, with a major increase in older users over time. In 2024, users aged 45–65+ represent the largest segments of its user base.

Source: Business2Community, IluvSugar

Hinge

Hinge maintains a relatively balanced age distribution, with the majority of users between 25–44. The app markets itself toward those seeking long-term relationships, which may explain its more stable user age mix.

Source: Helplama
