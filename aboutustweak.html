About Us

We’re a group of students creating a website that takes a closer look at the world of dating apps. Our project dives into user experiences, challenges, and trends through real data and research. By presenting surveys, interviews, and insights, we aim to give viewers a clear picture of how dating apps are really used — so you can see for yourself what’s working and what’s not. Have thoughts or questions? Contact us — we’d love to hear your perspective!
